VLSI Design Lab

EE6350 · Columbia University

Welcome to the home page of EE6350 VLSI Design Lab for Spring 2025. This graduate course focuses on the design, simulation, layout, verification and tape-out of an IC design. Our industrial sponsor is funding the fabrication of the chips in a 65nm CMOS foundry technology. The foundry fabricates the chips that students subsequently test in the Fall.
